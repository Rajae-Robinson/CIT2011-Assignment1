My Strengths

  1. Critical Thinking

    I am an excellent problem solver and a great thinker. As a result, I enjoy Programming as it allows me to solve problems. I have been developing this trait in the last five years by constantly seeking out new problems to solve particularly those that require use of computer algorithms.

  2. Organized

    I am highly organized. I need to keep track of tasks that I need to be done because I have a packed schedule. Over the past five years, I have been developing this trait by having a journal were I make note and keep track of my tasks.

  3. Creativity

    I have always been a creative person. I love learning and many different topics interest me. I have been developing this trait in the past five years my reading various books and exposing myself to different types of music and art.


"Twelve Pillars" Book Summary

Chapter 4

Chapter four is all about the importance of goak-setting. I learned to create short-term, mid-term and long-term goals and have a plan for achieving those goals. Also, it is best to take small steps daily towards the completion of those goals.

Chapter 5

Chapter five taught me that it is important to value my time and not waste it. As such, I will spend more time on activities that help improve my life.

Chapter 6

Chapter six enlightened me on the importance of spending time with successful and progressive people. It is important for me to choose my friends wisely as they have an influence on me.

Chapter 7

Chapter seven explained that I should aspire to be a life-long learner. Education doesn't end after university or high school.

Chapter 8

Chapter eight taught be that I should be a person of talent and virtue if I wish to be recognised and have influence on other people.
